Statistical Methods Implemented

Primary Analysis

Greenland & Longnecker (1992) - Generalized least squares regression for dose-response meta-analysis with correlated within-study estimates. This is the gold-standard method for dose-response meta-analysis.

Between-Study Variance (τ²)

  • DerSimonian-Laird (1986) - Method of moments estimator (default)
  • REML (Viechtbauer 2005) - Restricted maximum likelihood (recommended)
  • Paule-Mandel (1982) - Averaging method of moments
  • Empirical Bayes - Shrinkage estimator

Confidence Intervals

  • Wald - Normal approximation (β ± 1.96×SE)
  • Hartung-Knapp-Sidik-Jonkman (HKSJ) - Improved small-sample performance (Hartung et al. 2008; Sidik & Jonkman 2003)
  • Profile Likelihood - Likelihood-based CI (recommended for small samples)
  • Bootstrap - Resampling method (Efron 1987)

Heterogeneity

  • Cochran's Q - Heterogeneity chi-square test (Cochran 1954)
  • I² Statistic - Proportion of variance due to heterogeneity (Higgins et al. 2003)
  • H² Statistic - Heterogeneity scaling factor

Publication Bias

  • Egger's Test - Linear regression test (Egger et al. 1997)
  • Begg's Test - Rank correlation test (Begg & Mazumdar 1994)
  • Macaskill's Test - Regression of treatment effect on precision (Macaskill et al. 2001)
  • Peters' Test - Modified regression for binary outcomes (Peters et al. 2006)
  • Trim and Fill - Non-parametric method (Duval & Tweedie 2000)

Model Selection

AIC (Akaike 1974), BIC (Schwarz 1978), Likelihood Ratio Test - Compare non-nested models
